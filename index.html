Citrus Coffee and Cocktail company is San Luis Obispo's newest coffee shop by day, cocktail bar by night. Our mission is to provide and upbeat, airy environment for students and young adults to socialize and study. We want to foster a welcoming environment to all clientele whether you are visiting Citrus to utilize our ample work space, or coming in for happy hour with friends.
